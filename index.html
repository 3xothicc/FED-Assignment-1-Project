About Us

In the heart of Chantebelle, Vallonia, the iconic Hotel Elie, a symbol of 1920s sophistication, stands proudly amidst progress. A fusion of Art Deco's sleek lines and Art Nouveau's organic forms, the hotel emerged as a testament to the city's evolution. A grand opening gala marked the union of tradition and progress, establishing Hotel Elie as a timeless sanctuary. Join us in our century-long journey by booking your stay today in this living testament to Chantebelle's vibrant history and modernity.
