About Us

In the vibrant heart of Chantebelle, Vallonia, amid the bustling echoes of progress resonating through cobbled streets, proudly stood the illustrious Hotel Elie—a testament to the grandeur and sophistication of its era. Emerging in the early 1920s, this architectural marvel mirrored the dynamic spirit of a city undergoing industrial transformation. As Chantebelle evolved into a metropolis, the need for a symbol of modernity and classic elegance became apparent. Hotel Elie, a fusion of Art Deco's sleek lines and Art Nouveau's organic forms, captivated locals during its construction. The grand opening gala marked a union of tradition and progress, making Hotel Elie not just a luxury haven but a living testament to the city's evolution—a timeless sanctuary amidst progress.
